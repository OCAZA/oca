We Are OCA

Welcome to OCA, a vibrant collective committed to the revival and promotion of Odisha’s artistic traditions. From the classical elegance of Odissi dance to the intricate craftsmanship of Pattachitra, we aim to showcase the richness of our culture to the world. Our group serves as a platform for artists, musicians, and cultural enthusiasts to collaborate and share their love for Odisha’s heritage. We believe in keeping the spirit of our ancestors alive by embracing innovation while staying true to our roots. Explore, engage, and experience the cultural renaissance of Odisha with us!
